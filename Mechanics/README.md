## Shoulder assembly
![plot](./Pictures/Shoulder.png)
The shoulder is the main part of  Armus Most of the work is done by this part. The first thing to assemble should be the acrylic base with the shafts support. After that bold the two motor support to the main support.
Afterwards put the motor on those little support and plug the shaft to them along with the 14 theet gear. The next step is to place the long shaft with the two big gear and the little box to put the bolt passing trew the bevel gear. Afterwards put the bevel gear between the two main gear And tight the whole with a bolt that goes trew the little box in the middle of the shaft.  
***PLA infill 15%-20%***

## Upper Arm assembly
![plot](./Pictures/Upper_Arm.png)
The Main three parts to assemble are the two parts of the humerus and the L part(the L part is the part that will be link to the shoulder in the final assembly). Afterwards you can mount the motor inside the humerus with the shaft and pass it trought the shaft support.And finally put the 16 theet gear on the shaft.    
***PLA infill 10%-15%***
